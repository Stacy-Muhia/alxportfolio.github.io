The Epic Eats Project

A world where every tap is an epic journey in flavor discovery

Description

Epic Eats is a web application dedicated to connecting health-conscious food enthusiasts with local restaurants that specialize in offering organic, fresh, and vegan delights. With Epic Eats, users can explore a diverse range of culinary options while supporting sustainability and making healthier dining choices

Inspiration

Frustrated by the limited options for online food ordering apps that truly focus on healthy and sustainable meals, we launched Epic Eats. We witnessed firsthand how difficult it was to find organic, fresh, and locally sourced vegan food readily available for ordering. With Epic Eats, we're not just building a platform â€“ we're building a community. We envision a future where thousands of people can conveniently enjoy delicious, plant-based meals while supporting local restaurants committed to responsible practices. Beyond fulfilling academic requirements, it's our passionate pursuit to empower consumers, nurture local businesses, and create a positive impact on our environment, one delicious bite at a time.
